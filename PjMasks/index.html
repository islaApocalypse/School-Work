Gekko

Gekko (real name: Greg) is the youngest member, one quarter of the PJ Masks & his vehicular method of travel is the Gekko-mobile. Gekko may be the youngest member of the team but he’s a strong and sticky master of camouflage! He’s ready to rush into danger and save the day with his super Gekko muscles. Always ready for action, Gekko can scale walls and with his Gekko-mobile he can travel underwater! Watch out baddies, here comes the mighty Gekko!

Abilities

  1. Super Gekko Muscles- Gives him the ability to lift extremely large & heavy objects.
  2. Super Lizard Grip- Can climb on walls and other surfaces like a gecko.
  3. Super Gekko Camoulflage- Can turn invisible.
  4. Super Lizard Water Run- Can run on water. This power has only been used once.
  5. Color-Changing- Can change colors of his costume, like an anole lizard.
  6. Super Gekko Shields- can summon two shields, which he can use to block incoming attacks or as projectiles.
  7. Amulet Symbol- When pressed, he can communicate with his spirit animal (a gecko).

CatBoy

Catboy (real name: Connor) is the leader, one quarter of the PJ Masks and His vehicular method of travel is the Cat-Car. Catboy is the leader of the trio, and the oldest too. Incredibly fast, amazingly agile, he can hear the quietest sounds across unbelievable distances!With these skills Catboy can outrun anyone and gives Night Ninja a challenge with his amazing leaps and acrobatic moves! His vehicle, the Cat-Car, gets the heroes from A to B in lightning speed!

Abilities

  1. Super Cat Speed- Which allows him to run extremely fast, faster than the eye can see. However, he can only use this speed for short amounts of time.
  2. Cat Speed Side Effect- A side effect of this power is that he generates lightning while running, which he can harness for other purposes.
  3. Super Cat Ears- He has enhanced hearing, allowing him to hear sounds that would otherwise be well outside his hearing range.
  4. Super Cat Jump- Another side effect of his super speed, he can jump much higher than a regular human.
  5. Super Cat Stripes- A power he gained which Catboy can shoot glowing blue energy stripes that can be used to tie up opponents or to grab/hold on to objects.
  6. Amulet Symbol- When pressed, he can communicate with his spirit animal (a cat).

Owlette

Owlette (real name: Amaya) is the only female member and one quarter of the team, the only team member capable of flight. Her vehicular method of travel is the Owl Glider. Owlette, with her sharp mind and super planning skills, is quick to act. She can fly, has amazing eyesight, and when she flaps her powerful wings, the bad guys are sent airborne! Also very sweet and cares for Connor and Greg and their safety. She's also helpful, friendly, and smart, but still naive.

Abilities

  1. Super Owl Eyes- Can see in the dark, and see things from very far away.
  2. Super Owl Wings- Her primary power; she can fly with her cape, like wings.
  3. Owl Wing Wind- Can create powerful gusts of wind with her owl wings.
  4. Super Owl Feathers- A power gained she can shoot energy feathers from her owl wings. These feathers come in various sizes, and can be used for multiple purposes, like projectiles, or to trap an opponent.
  5. Super Feather Shield- Can create a large shield using her owl feathers.
  6. Super Owl Swoop- Can perform a maneuver while flying.
  7. Amulet Symbol- When pressed, she can communicate with her spirit animal (a pigeon).

PJ Robot

Friend of the PJ Masks, this miniature inventor is the keeper of HQ and unlocks the heroes new superpowers! PJ Robot is small, cute and has his own language of mechanical clicks, beeps and fizzes. PJ robot aids the PJ Masks from Headquarters or in the field. originally invented by Romeo as part of a plan to infiltrate HQ. Romeo intentionally made PJ Robot cute and friendly, so that way the heroes would like him and take him to their HQ. However, PJ Robot began to genuinely like the PJ Masks, and eventually betrayed Romeo to join them.

Abilities

  1. Multiple Weapons- PJ Robot has at least four weapons installed in him, and can fight alongside the PJ Masks if needed.
  2. Remote Control- PJ Robot can remote control HQ's rocket ship function.
  3. Magician- PJ Robot knows magic tricks and juggling.
  4. Musician- PJ Robot can play music to wake people up.
  5. Flight- PJ Robot has the ability to hover above the ground, and presumed to fly at high speeds & altitudes due to its wings. But additional details of its ability are currently unknown.

Luna Girl

Luna Girl is a thief and a mysterious girl that uses a moon-shaped device to control things and has a swarm of moths to help her with her evil deeds. Luna Girl wants all the daytime things for herself and she’ll have a terrible tantrum if she doesn’t get her way! Also can be very selfish, malicious, manipulative and stingy. And has never been afraid of committing devious and somewhat twisted acts.

Abilities

  1. Luna Board- Luna Girl owns a hoverboard-like object called the "Luna Board" which she uses as transportation (on occasion she allows her moths to carry her places). She is able to summon the hoverboard by shouting out its name.
  2. Luna Magnet- She owns a "Luna Magnet" which can fire a tractor beam to attract objects she wants or traps people in a moon bubble
  3. Luna Wand- Her Luna Magnet can become extra powerful when Luna Girl inserts a harvest Moon Crystal into it, and it turns into a "Luna Wand". If Luna Girl has her Luna Wand and is on the moon, she can grow large clumps of moon crystals to block or trap people and even heal the moon if it is cracked. Additionally, her Luna Wand can track Gekko when he uses his Super Gekko Camouflage, almost like a kind of heat sensor.

Night Ninja

Night ninja is the leader and the authority figure of the Ninjalinos. A Splat-Sticking, back-flipping Ninja wants to show everyone that he’s the best at everything! Night Ninja rarely gets involved in a battle as he summons his henchmen, the Ninjalinos, to do his dirty work for him. He is incredibly arrogant and haughty.

Abilities

  1. Sticky Splat- Uses colorful and gooey objects known as "Sticky Splat" to pin down the PJ Masks and hinder their moving abilities.
  2. Martial Arts- Acrobatics: He is very agile and can perform spins, back flips and high jumps at ease. On occasion, he will use a move called the "mega triple punch".
  3. Smoke Bombs- Are used by Night Ninja so he can disappear and reappear wherever he wants. He runs off in the smoke and he doesn't really teleport.
  4. Ninja Fingers- He and his Ninjalinos have a power called “ninja fingers” which gives them the power to move any object of any size with only their fingers.
  5. Teleportation- He is able to teleport small objects such as sticky splat and scrolls that he's stolen.

Romeo

A marvel of mechanical mischief, meet mad scientist Romeo. With all his amazing inventions he plans to take over the world! This child prodigy plans on total world domination by reaching his goals with robots, inventions or anything to make the world love him. Romeo is extremely intelligent for his age, but sadly, like many about as young as he is, Romeo fails to see the sagely distinction between knowledge and wisdom, consistently confusing either for both. He isn't very physically combative, nor is he particularly arrogant about his intellect, but is very bent and keen on taking over the world and has an ego to prove that he's the greatest inventor. He has an intense dislike for the PJ Masks, but isn't extremely violent with them. He usually accepts his fate and flees when he knows he is defeated.

Abilities

  1. High Intelligence- Romeo doesn't possess the acrobatic and physical agility of Night Ninja and his Ninjalinos, nor the assistance of moths like Luna Girl, but he uses the power of knowledge and invention to help him take over the world.

Wolfy Kids

The Wolfy Kids, also known as the Wolfys or Wolfies are a trio of werewolf siblings. The members of the pack include: Howler, Rip, and Kevin. “Yip! Yip! Yarooo!” The Wolfy Kids are always out to cause trouble by racing around town, messing things up, and causing havoc with their combined super-sonic yowls!

Abilities

  1. Sonic Howl- All three can create sonic waves with their howl.
  2. Superhuman Agility- All three Wolfies are quite agile, being able to leap over tall objects. They can run on hands and feet for extra speed.
