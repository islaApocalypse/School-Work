(CIS) Members

The Confederacy was led by the former Jedi Count Dooku of Serenno, who served as its Head of State. In addition, the Confederacy was secretly supported by several corporations, including the Trade Federation, headed by Viceroy Nute Gunray; the InterGalactic Banking Clan, headed by Chairman San Hill; the Techno Union, headed by Foreman Wat Tambor; the Geonosians, headed by Prime Leader Poggle the Lesser; the Commerce Guild, headed by Presidente Shu Mai; the Corporate Alliance, headed by Magistrate Passel Argente; and the Retail Caucus.

Confederacy of Independent Systems

The Confederacy of Independent Systems (24 BBY–19 BBY), abbreviated to CIS, and also known as the Separatist Alliance or the Separatist State, was an unrecognized breakaway state that fought against the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars. Formed by thousands of star systems from the Mid Rim to the Outer Rim that broke away from the Republic, the Confederacy was founded on the grounds of excessive taxation and corruption within the Galactic Senate and general feeling of dissatisfaction towards and neglect by the Core Worlds, which contained some of the wealthiest and most prestigious planets in the galaxy. The Separatist Crisis that began in 24 BBY led to a political crisis in which tensions escalated between the newly-formed Confederacy and the reigning Republic. In 22 BBY, the Confederacy and the Republic went to war using their respective military forces—the droid army and the Republic clone army—resulting in a pan-galactic conflict for the first time in a millennium. The following three years saw the Clone Wars raging across the galaxy with the Separatists rapidly attempting to bring the Republic to its knees.
