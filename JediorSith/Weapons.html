Types of Weapons

Within our vast universe there is a unique assortment of offensive tools. Directed-energy weapons (DEW) are most common, with the most types referred to as lasers or blasters. Next projectile weapons, also referred to as slugthrowers, appear in various forms: cycler rifles and the bowcaster (which is a crossbow-like weapon) are two examples. Rockets & Missles are also used admist war with proton torpedoes, concussion and discord missiles. Other explosives include grenades. Traditonally there is also melee weapons such as staffs, swords, batons and whips with the most notable of these is the Lightsaber.

Where to Acquire

Most weapons of the galaxy can be purchased from simple vendors or cantines. Though there are other means of aquisition as well such as smuggling, raiding and the calmer route of trading. Some weapons are acquired differently and through special cases like the jedi & sith lightsabers.
